Admission Requirements

Prospective students seeking admission into Junior Secondary School (JSS) or Senior Secondary School (SSS) are required to provide:

  • Completed Admission Application Form
  • Recent Passport Photograph
  • Photocopy of Birth Certificate
  • Last School Result/Testimonial
  • Transfer Letter (if applicable)
  • Entrance Examination Participation

Admission Process

  1. Obtain the Admission Form from the school office or online portal.
  2. Complete and submit the form with required documents.
  3. Sit for the Entrance Examination.
  4. Successful candidates will be invited for an interview.
  5. Admission Letter will be issued upon approval.

Entrance Examination

Entrance examinations are conducted periodically throughout the academic session. Parents/Guardians will be notified of examination dates after application submission.

School Resumption

Admitted students are expected to resume at the beginning of the academic term as stated in the admission letter.
